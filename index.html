Here you will find out about Domino squad and Comandos!

Comander Cody

Comander Cody was Obi Wan's platoon comander. He fought with the 212s. When order 66 came in he ordered his men to shoot down Obi Wan. When the Empire rose Cody became an imperial stormtrooper captain for his successful missions.

Captain Rex

Captain Rex was the Captain of the 501st petalion. He fought asside Anakin Skyalker and Ahsoka Tano. When order 66 came in he dissobeyed itb as he had removed his inhibiter chip. Then he joined up with Gregor and Wolf and lived on an old AT TE. Until the Ghost crew came and Rex joined the rebellion.

Comander Gree

Comander Gree was an effective Comander. He lead the Kashyyk battle alongside Yoda. He obeyed every single order without any complaining. But when order 66 came in he tried to kill Yoda but instead got his head de-capitated by Yoda.

Comander Wolf

Comander Wolf lead the Wolf Pack alongside Master Plo Koon. He had removed his inhibbiter chip so he dissobeyed order 66 when it came in. He then joined up with Captain Rex and Gregor.

Captain Blackout

We unfortunatly don't know much about Blackout, but we know that he lead the mission to pass the seperatist blockade in the Cloaked Ship with Anakin and Obi Wan.
