Ranks

Each Clone Trooper had a rank here you will see the ranks

CAPTAIN: The Captain would be the one who would lead his own platoon into battle and would come up with most of the plans. The most famous Captain is: Captain Rex.
LIEUTENENT: Lieutenents were able to give few comands along side there platoon leaders.
COMANDER: The Comanders were better rank than Captains they didn't fight along side captains tho as they had there own platoon too. The most famous Comander(s) were: Comander Cody and Comander Wolf.
SNOW TROOPER: Snow Troopers were regular clones but they were trained to fight in the snow and wore Snow armor.
AIRBORN TROOPER: Airborn Troopers were part of the 212th legion lead by Obi Wan Kenobi and Comander Cody.
ARF TROOPERS: Arf Troopers were the Scouts. They were called Arf Troopers during the Grand Army of the Republic but when the Empire rose it was changed to Scout Trooper.

REGULAR PHASE 1 TROOPER: The Regular Phase 1 Clones were just regular clone they faught during the biggining of the Clone Wars and Star Wars Ep 2 Attack Of The Clones.
REGULAR PHASE 2 TROOPER: The Regular Phase 2 Clones were just normal soldiers. All they did was obey the Captain or Comander's orders.
COMANDOS: Comandos were the ELITE troopers. There wasn't any better rank than this. The most famous Comano Unit(s) is: Beta Squad and Delta Squad.
ARC TROOPERS: Arc Troopers were High Rank troopers. They were just below COMANDOS. The most famous Arc Unit is: Domino Squad.
Shock Trooper: The Shock Trooper was a police type clone trooper who was always on Corruscant.
